RIDE ZANSKAR | THE HIDDEN KINGDOM 11 Days & 10 Nights

PACKAGE OVERVIEW
  • Day 1 ARRIVE - LEH

    When you’re about to reach Ladakh, the very first glimpse of this snow laden heaven brings a twinkle to your eyes! Just imagine looking down on a silent, placid, greenish blue lake – beautiful, isn’t it? Or imagine the sight of magnificent mountains greeting you – that was a for your welcome . There's nothing more enchanting than nature's handiwork in Ladakh... And then as you enter Leh, our representative will whisk you to your hotel, rest of the day is spent in acclimatization for your healthier Ladakh trip, free from any acute altitude sickness.

  • Day 2 LEH – KARGIL

    HIGHWAY DAY !

    After fixing accessories and gearing up soon, we will kick start towards Kargil and today the route is smooth enough with two passes to cross Fotula (13,478ft) and Namikala (12,139 ft) and While romancing with few breath-taking villages and throwing candies to the kids who were excited to see the biker group and enroute we visit few famous places like Pathar Saab , River confluence at Nimmo, Moon land at Lamayuru, Maitrya Buddha at Mulbik etc. By the evening we’ll reach Kargil.


  • Day 3 KARGIL - RANGDUM

    READY FROR RIDE

    Another great planned day soon ahead going through Suru valley the best picturesque palace with lot of farming fields and ride along Glacier Mountains. Today will be more broken roads as compare to yesterday with couple of water crossing as well where twin peaks of Nun (7135 m) and Kun (7087 m) are the biggest and main attraction in the valley would be seen and the views throughout the journey was brilliant which changing on every gear shifting, what ever direction we see it's so scenic and eye catching and on arrival visit the rich ancient monastery of random if your not more tired. Overnight Guesthouse.


  • Day 4 RANGDUM – PADUM
    NUBRA VALLEY

    Tuff But Not Challenging !

    Good Morning Zanskar ! Yet another epic day for kick back on some raw routes. Another pass to climb up for the wide welcome to Padum (head quite of Zanskar) @ Pensi la (14000 ft). The highest point between Kargil & Zanskar you find yourself surrounded by glaciers, small lakes and Marmots are frequently seen and the beauty was such that you can’t resist yourself to take few shots and from here onwards the real beauty of Zanskar become apparent. Without taking much time we roll down and ride onn the full of dirt upto the outskirts of padum where you are greeted by a tarmac road after riding for about 80 km on dirt trials. Padum is a small town with all amenities like hotel, restaurant, Internet café etc.

  • Day 6 RANGDUM - DHA

    INN ARYAN VALLEY

    After a energizing early breakfast we leave Rangdum towards Aryans valley via Kargil & Hamboting la (15202 ft). From kargil onwards we chose a different route vai Batalik sector & Dha Bema which is recently open for tourist. Narrow mountainous roads welcome us to Dha Hanu . Overnight in Aryan valley Camp


  • Day 7 DHA - LEH

    BACK TO TOWN

    Hope the day is quite warm enough for riding along the Indus via some small pretty villages enroute like Skurbuchan, Domkhar and we hit back NH1 at Khaltsi where we stop for Lunch and we were again on a Smooth road to Leh , enroute visit Alchi Monastery and reach Leh by evening for better network connections.?


  • Day 8 LEH - PANGONG

    A BIG START

    Today we were riding to one of the most famous high altitude lake which lies on Leh - China border and it won’t be a finish able lake because Lake is about 162KMS long and 60% of it likes in China knows as yumdok lake of China. Ride to pangong Lake is about twisting and turning to the spectacular Changla pass for view the straight out snow peaks of Himalaya from 5360 m with a cup of hot tea and it would be a snowy pass if your crossing in April , May , October. By spending not much time we move ahead towards our destination while encountering some small nomad villages. A scenic ride will end by evening on lake side with some chill beers.

    ANOTHER SUPERB DAY

    The epic journey goes ahead towards Nubra Valley and deep part of the valley is the Border between India - Pakistan.
