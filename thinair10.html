MANALI-LEH -SRINAGAR | LADAKH 11 Days & 10 Nights

PACKAGE OVERVIEW
  • Day 1 ARRIVE - MANALI

    WARM WELCOME

    Arrive in Manali and check in the said Hotel /Guest House, where team LBR is excited to meet our 2020 Riders from different corners of the world. Get rest for an hours and we will meet for ground briefing about road to Ladakh while clearing each and every one doubt. In evening LBR guide is excited to take your through Manali town for perfect dinner and close introduction with new friends of this trip.


  • Day 2 MANALI – KELONG | 120 KMS

    READY TO RIDE

    GEAR UP ! Its time to meet your motorcycles, Get your keys, saddle on your motorcycles and check your bikes for minor adjustment like mirror, breaks, handle bar set up etc. Your on Chapter road to Ladakh which is one of the most popular adventure ride in Himalayas . We start out trip with no delays towards Kelong via busy Rotang Pass. The low speeds and super twisty roads while enjoying the relaxing views is the starting . Soon after 2 hours we will be on top of Rotang Pass (3,980m) and take a pause for long breath for full view of Himalayas , enjoy the view with great company and proceed down hill for end the scenic ride in Kelong with chill beers together.


  • Day 3 KELONG - SERCHU | 108 KMS

    STUNNING ROADS

    After breakfast kick start your machines for todays ride towards Serchu, Ride will be quite interesting and adventurous via some eye catching villages like Jispa and Darcha. Baralacha-la pass(4,980m) is the highest we were crossing today to be at our destination and few water crossing is part of today.


  • Day 4 LONGEST DAY | 250 KMS
    NUBRA VALLEY

    SERCHU - LEH

    Ready to get the adventure back on the road of popular Leh – Manali. Today you will strike out for the Ladakh riding through bare mountains crossing of Lachung la pass (16,616 ft ) and Nakeela Pass (15,547 ft ) while looping up through Gata Loops and having the ride of a lifetime on more plains and the Challenging Taklangla Pass (17,480 ft, world’s second highest Motorable pass). We’ll soon roll into the Heart of Leh city and have a good shower on arrival at hotel.

  • Day 5 LEISURE DAY LEH - LOCAL
    NUBRA VALLEY

    Back in Network Zone ! Today we keep it short on Motorbikes and more on foot to explore market. We were going for a quick ride in few different direction for visits some famous cultural part and monument and later walk to market for check some good teste.

  • Day 6 EAST LADAKH | 150 KMS

    LEH - PANGONG

    Today we were riding to one of the most famous high altitude lake which lies on Leh - China border and it was not a finighable lake because Lake is about 162KMS long and 60% of it likes in China knows as yumdok lake of China. Ride to pangong Lake is about twisting and turning to the spectacular Change pass for view the straight out snow peaks of Himalaya from 5360 m with a cup of hot tea and it would be a snowy pass if your crossing in April , May , October. By spending not much time we move ahead towards our destination while encountering some small nomad villages. A scenic ride will end by evening on lake side with some chill beers.


  • Day 7 EAST TO NORTH

    PANGONG - NUBRA

    The epic journey goes ahead towards Nubra Valley a deep part of the valley is the Border between India - Pakistan. Ride goes on same yesterday route about 1 hour and direction take palace towards north ladakh and we believe its much warmer then yesterday with some smooth roads straight roads along the shayok Village. Unknow flowing of water over roads is common on this area and might we have a good chance for water crossing and some time makes block as well which is very rare. We have a good chance for ride some double camp camel which exist no where in world but some of them left here because of being a part of Ancient silk route trade. The charming village offers good stays and we will grip out best.

    lake side.

  • Day 8 NUBRA - LEH | 120 KMS

    ACHIEVING DAY

    Good Morning Nubra Valley ! Today is the last day and we were crossing Khardongla Pass ( World Highest Motor-able Road ) a gate way to nubra valley or Leh. As soon as packed we kick start from Hundar towards pretty hill lock Diskit monestry with Stunning Buddha statue which is the blessed part of the trip. Todays route is a busy road because most of the riders were sporting in this route and about that playing of army convoy trucks with black smokes is common but still we wish for less and keep pushing up to the top for celebrate the Highest ride. Its time to show your ride with epic selfies in the back drop of Khardong la where we won’t suggest for spent more time due to the high altitude. Back to leh for celebrate the last night in one of the best restaurant of town.

  • Day 9 LEH – KARGIL | 220 KMS

    SMOOTH HIGHWAY

    After fixing accessories and gearing up soon, we will kick start towards Kargil and today the route is smooth enough with two passes to cross Fotula (13,478ft) and Namikala (12,139 ft) and While romancing with few breath-taking villages and throwing candies to the kids who were excited to see the biker group enroute we visit few famous places like Pathar Saab. River confluence at Nimmo, Moon land at Lamayuru, Maitrya Buddha at Mulbik etc. By the evening we’ll reach Kargil.


  • Day 10 KARGIL - SRINAGAR | 202 KMS

    Today is the Last day of our tour and well goes through Drass (2nd coldest inhabited place after Siberia) and where we explore war memorial and thereafter by throttling the same route we reach Zujila pass (11,575 ft) mean while we enter Srinagar will reach Sonamarag a beauty itself just nestled at the bottom of ZUJILA pass and crossing awesome villages well in the Kashmir. Overnight in house boat for the best farewell
