LEH-MANALI | EPIC RIDE 9 Days & 8 Nights

PACKAGE OVERVIEW
  • Day 1 ARRIVE LEH

    MOST AWAITED DAY

    A short journey of about 1 hour flight from Delhi bring you to the beautiful mountains of Leh (3000 mtr amsl )the basement of our tour, LBR representative will be waiting for you at Kushok Bakula Rinpochey airport Leh for your warm welcome. The representative will further proceed you to the Hotel and drop you with the small tips about getting acclimatise to high altitude for your adventurous trip.

  • Day 2 LEH - PANGONG | 150 KMS

    A BIG START
    This is the day we have all been waiting for, check out and gear up, will load the baggage in support van. We head towards LBR garage for a detailed rider briefing where you meet your bikes and do select the best beast for your adventurous trip.We are located out from market which is perfect location for a quick test ride. Today we were riding to one of the most famous high altitude lake, the Pangong Lake which lies on Indo – China border and is 162 kms long, the 60% of it lies in China where it is know as Yumdok lake. The road to pangong Lake is about twisting and turning with a spectacular Changla pass to cross, enjoy the view from the top with a cup of hot tea and even you will find the if you are crossing in April, May and October months. By spending not much time we move ahead towards our destination encountering some small nomadic villages. A scenic ride will end by evening on lake side with some chilled beers.


  • Day 3 PANGONG - NUBRA | 160 KMS

    CRUSHING NORTH
    Today’s epic ride goes towards Nubra Valley and deep part of the valley is the Border between India - Pakistan. Ride goes on same route for about 1 hour and after Durbuk village direction take palace towards northern Ladakh and we believe its much warmer then other parts of Ladakh with some smooth and open straight roads along the Shayok river. We encounter unexpected streams of water over roads in these route where we get a good chance to show our riding skills for crossing and sometimes even it makes the road block though it is very rare. Riders has a good opportunity for double hump camel ride which exist nowhere in India except here in Nubra Valley as some of them are left here because Ladakh being a part of Ancient silk route trade. Night stay at Hunder, the charming village which offer good stays and we will grip our best.

  • Day 4 NUBRA - LEH | 120 KMS
    NUBRA VALLEY

    After a refreshing breakfast, Ride your machines for a uphill adventure towards Northwards Ladakh by leaving the town crowd behind, tracks were in gravel & paved and snowy mountains takes up to 18,380 ft. for Khardong-la pass (The Highest Motor able Road in The world) Riders goals is here and with some posses & pictures and soon interesting landscape ride take us to Hundar Nubra Valley

  • Day 5 LEISURE DAY
    NUBRA VALLEY

    LEH - LOCAL
    Back in Network Zone! Today we keep it short on Motorbikes and more on foot. In the morning we were going for a quick ride in different nearby direction to explore some cultural part of Ladakh with some famous monasteries and monuments and soon after the ride , afternoon we take a walk to explore beautiful Leh market.

  • Day 6 LEH - SERCHU | 250 KMS

    LIVE TO RIDE
    Today’s Ride goes through Challenging Taklang-la Pass (17,480 ft), Enjoying the riding on more plains. Crossing Nakeela Pass (15,547 ft) and Lachulung-la (16,616 ft) and by looping through Gata loops. We nestle our stay in Serchu

  • Day 7 SERCHU – KELONG | 110 KMS

    CHANGING LANDSCAPE
    We leave Serchu in the morning and by riding in an awesome landscape we soon Leave UT Ladakh behind while knocking Himachal Pradesh. And by crossing Baralachala pass (16,500 ft) encountering small villages like Darcha and Jispa we spent our nigh in the city of Keylong.

  • Day 8 KELONG - MANALI | 120 KMS

    EPIC RIDE
    Today is the Last day of our tour and we trace our route via famous Rothang Pass (13,060 ft) the pass where someone feels quite high in the sky and where weather condition remains highly unpredictable and by rolling down from Rothang pass towards Manali one can see the full view of Manali and in the evening get ready for big farewell.

  • Day 9 TOUR ENDS

    Last day and yet another pass is left to cross that is Taglang la, world second highest motor able road. Vast open landscapes to ride for famous Taglang-la and You can enjoy the great session of gravel and most griped tarmac as well. Reach Leh town by noon, drop your bikes at thinair garage check hotel and fresh up for farewell night.
