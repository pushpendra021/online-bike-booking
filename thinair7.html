LEH LADAKH WHOLE TOUR 10 Days & 9 Nights

PACKAGE OVERVIEW
  • Day 1 Arrive Leh

    When you’re about to reach Ladakh, the very first glimpse of this snow laden heaven brings a twinkle to your eyes! Just imagine looking down on a silent, placid, greenish blue lake – beautiful, isn’t it? Or imagine the sight of magnificent mountains greeting you – that was a for your welcome . There's nothing more enchanting than nature's handiwork in Ladakh... And then as you enter Leh, our representative will whisk you to your hotel, rest of the day is spent in acclimatization for your healthier Ladakh trip, free from any acute altitude sickness.

  • Day 2 Leh – Dhahanu

    after having your breakfast ,thinair team will be introduce you and get ready for the zeal of adventures bike riding on the land of lama's to visit the tourist places around Leh like the Hall of Fame which salutes the spirit of the Indian martyrs. Post that we crossed the highway (NH1) of Ladakh from where we see the meeting point of Zanskar and Indus . and ride towards south west of Ladakh into the heart of pure Indo Aryan people. where you will find another scenic beauty , beautiful culture of warm hearted people of aryn valley . the thinair rider STANZIN where he belongs he will introduce everything about dahanu

  • Day 3 Dhahanu – Leh via Chiktan

    after and great view of dahanu so lets roll back to leh via beautiful valley called "Chiktan" and along the roads find lot of kids for cheers up and while romancing the tarmac roads of Namkila & Fotula Pass we will have spectacular view of Lamayuru Monastery along the back drop of Moon Land and by witnessing it we will in the leh town by the evening for a stay with better network connectivity.

  • Day 4 Leh – Nubra
    NUBRA VALLEY

    After a refreshing breakfast, Ride your machines for a uphill adventure towards Northwards Ladakh by leaving the town crowd behind, tracks were in gravel & paved and snowy mountains takes up to 18,380 ft. for Khardong-la pass (The Highest Motor able Road in The world) Riders goals is here and with some posses & pictures and soon interesting landscape ride take us to Hundar Nubra Valley

  • Day 5 Nubra – Pangong
    NUBRA VALLEY

    Today we venture on the same route till Khalsar and from there by turning our left indicator our ride goes towards eastern part of ladakh. Riding up to Pangong Lake (15000 ft) along Shayok river trails with mesmerizing landscape and water crossing and playing on gravel roads is common in this roads, this ride will take you to the deep enchanting nature of open roads.

  • Day 6 Pangong – Leh

    By catching the beautiful view of tranquil lake in the morning sun-rays we’ll set up our bike for Leh again while throttling through Chang la 17590 ft (third highest motor-able road) the scenery is simply fantastic, a little bit of off roading , crossing small streams of water and thundering over rocks we climb changla, and by enjoying the hot steaming black tea and by riding down from changla, you can zip through some narrow roads while enjoying the breeze and the beautiful views and soon we will enter Leh.

  • Day 7 Leh – Tsomoriri

    Today’s ride goes through Indus River till up to Mahi Bridge and by switching our right indicator we leave the valley to enter the heart of Rupshu, the most isolated part of Ladakh. We promise this would be the best part of your ride through some of the best nature trails and do chat with colors in the wilderness of Changthang, By taking sandy and rough track with our off riding skill we reach the second most important lake in Ladakh, the Tsomoriri lake 15075 ft above mean sea level. Explore lake, visit Korzok monastery and look out for high altitude wildlife. Overnight camp along lake side.

  • Day 8 Tsomoriri – Tsokar

    As the sun rises, you will be head out from Tsomoriri to Tsokar. We named it as nomadic ride through Rupshu plains. The terrain will consist of dusty roads along stunning landscapes and it will be your best skill day. Reach by noon and visit 100 years old Thukzay monastery and Tsokar is also know as one of the best birding spot for bird lovers. Overnight at Tsokar

  • Day 9 Tsokar – Leh

    Last day and yet another pass is left to cross that is Taglang la, world second highest motor able road. Vast open landscapes to ride for famous Taglang-la and You can enjoy the great session of gravel and most griped tarmac as well. Reach Leh town by noon, drop your bikes at LBR garage and check hotel and fresh up for farewell night.

  • Day 10 Fly Back

    Today will be your departure day. You will be transferred to the airport to catch your flight back home
